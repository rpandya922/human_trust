Instructions

You will be working with simulated slot machines. Each machine, or "Slot", has a fixed payout and fixed probability of giving you that payout each time. Your goal is to maximize your total payout.

You will be working with 4 different robots colored Red, Blue, Yellow, and Green. Each robot has a different way of learning, and you will collaborate with each of them in a different casino. Each casino has different slot machines.

You can get up to a $0.50 bonus! The bonus you get will be proportional to your payout across all casinos.
