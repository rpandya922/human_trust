Instructions

You will be working with simulated slot machines. Each machine, or "Arm", has a fixed payout and fixed probability of giving you that payout each time. Your goal is to maximize your total payout.

You will be working with 4 different robots colored Red, Blue, Yellow, and Green. Each robot has a different way of learning, and you will get to collaborate with each of them on a different set of slot machines.

You will first get to explore one scenario on your own. Click Next to begin.
